What ZTech Offers...

With our application, you can access a series of information that will allow you to effectively take care of your household plants.

Specific Information about Your Favorite Plants

Thanks to our database of household plants, you can receive specific care information for any type of plant you have in your hands.

Dedicated Sensors

We allow plant owners to monitor the environmental conditions of their plants in real-time, including temperature, soil moisture, and potassium levels.

Increased Confidence for Users

As plant owners, having specific guidance and advice for each type of plant they own will give them more confidence and skills in care.
